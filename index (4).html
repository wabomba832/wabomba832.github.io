    Igredients and procedures on how to cook.

    INGREDIENTS

      for the beef stew:
  • 1. 500g beef
  • 2. 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 3. 1 chopped onion
  • 4. 2 tomatoes chopped
  • 5. 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 6. 1 beef cube
  • 7. salt
  • 8. fresh coriader
      • for the ugali
  • 1. 2 cups maize flour
  • 2. 4 cups water
  • 3. mwiko

    • PROCEDURE

      for the beef stew
  • 1. Boil the beef in a pot with little water and salt until tender(about 30-45 minutes)
  • 2. In another pot ,heat the oil and saute the onions until golden brown.
  • 3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute
  • 4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they form a thick paste.
  • 5. Add boiled beef
  • 6. Season with beef cube and salt.
  • 7. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until flavors blend and stew thickens.
  • 8. garnish with chopped coriander.
      • for ugali
  • 1. Boil water in the sufuria.
  • 2. Gradually add maize flour
  • 3. Keep stirring and smashing until the mixture thickens.
  • 4. Cover it and let cook on low heat.
  • 5. Serve hot with the beef stew.

    • MATOKE STEW

    Ingredients and procedure on how to cook.

      INGREDIENTS
    <
  • 1. 6-8 matoke,slicced.
  • 2. 2 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 3. 1 chopped onion
  • 4. 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 5. 1 teaspoon garlic paste.
  • 6. salt to taste.
  • 7. fresh coriander
      • PROCEDURE
  • 1. Heat oil in the sufuria and fry onions until golden brown.
  • 2. Add garlic paste and stir for few seconds.
  • 3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and saucy.
  • 4. Add chopped matoke, mix well
  • 5. Add salt, beef and beef water just enough to cover the matoke.
  • 6. Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes until matoke is tender and stew is thick.
  • 7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
